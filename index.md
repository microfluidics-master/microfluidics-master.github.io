The **Microfluidics degree (Master 2)** — supported by the *Institut Pierre‑Gilles de Gennes (IPGG)* — is an **interdisciplinary** program oriented toward **innovation and industrial applications**. It offers one of the most direct routes to engage with the **microfluidics start‑up ecosystem**.

The degree is integrated within two host master programs:

- *Physics of Complex Systems* (Sorbonne Université, Université Paris Cité, Université Paris‑Saclay) — for physics‑oriented students;
- *Materials Science & Engineering* (Université PSL) — for chemistry/biology‑oriented students.
