## Transcend Scientific Disciplines

The Microfluidics program is a focused **interdisciplinary** plan of study. Students take **mandatory and elective** courses across micro‑technologies, natural and physical sciences (**biology, chemistry, physics**), and **instrumentation**. The program culminates in a **Master’s project and thesis**.

A distinctive feature is the integration of **IPGG’s interdisciplinary environment** into a unified curriculum that combines coursework, lab classes, and a research project adapted to each student's interests.
